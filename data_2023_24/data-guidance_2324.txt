National Tutoring Programme
2023/24 Academic year

Description

Summary of the delivery data on the National Tutoring Programme (NTP) for academic year 2023/24. This includes statistics on courses started by pupils and school participation.

Coverage

Delivery data since the start of the NTP covers courses delivered to pupils in the full 2020/21, 2021/22 and 2022/23 academic years, and the 2023/24 year up to 5th October.

For the 2023/24 academic year, the data covers the cumulative number of courses started by pupils and school participation up to 5th October 2023.

The statistics and data for delivery cover England, including regional and local authority breakdowns. The data is also broken down by school phase.

File formats and conventions

The files are in .csv format.

Footnotes:

1. Figures may not always sum to totals exactly, due to rounding.
2. Delivery of tutoring in Independent Special Schools and centrally arranged for Looked After Children is not measured in the school census and is therefore not included here.
3. This data contains estimates of courses started by pupils up to 5th October within the 2023/24 academic year based on the Autumn 2023 School Census. See methodology (https://explore-education-statistics.service.gov.uk/methodology/national-tutoring-programme-statistics-methodology) for further information.
4. School participation is identified based on responses to the School Census. See methodology (https://explore-education-statistics.service.gov.uk/methodology/national-tutoring-programme-statistics-methodology) for further information.
5. Total schools includes all open schools with pupils aged between 5 and 16, as defined in the methodology (https://explore-education-statistics.service.gov.uk/methodology/national-tutoring-programme-statistics-methodology).
